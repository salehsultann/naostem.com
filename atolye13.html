Welcome to Exploring College Options! Programs typically last one hour followed by 30 minutes of individual school break-out sessions. Students, we encourage your parent(s) or guardian(s) to watch the program with you, and if you have a friend who you think might be interested, please direct him/her to this website for registration information. Each attendee will need to submit their own registration information. For a complete listing of our spring events, please visit our website. Exploring College Options encourages all persons with disabilities to participate in these programs. For every session, live audio-to-text transcription will be made available. If you anticipate needing any type of accommodation or have questions about the virtual access provided, please contact Mark Dudley at mark.dudley(at)duke(dot)edu or 919-684-0166.
